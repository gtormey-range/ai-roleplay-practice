AI Roleplay: Managing a Former Peer

What you’ll do

In this interactive roleplay, you’ll step into a realistic conversation with Raina — your former peer, now your direct report. The AI responds in real time as a real teammate might.

Goal: practice setting new norms, clarifying expectations, and handling role changes with empathy and clarity.

Helper Questions

  • How will you acknowledge the shift from peer → manager?
  • What expectations do you need to reset today?
  • Where could friction show up, and how will you surface it?

Tips

  • Lead with curiosity. Ask before telling.
  • State expectations as behaviors and outcomes.
  • Close with agreements, owners, and next steps.
